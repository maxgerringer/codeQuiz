Welcome to codeQuiz!

codeQuiz is a ten question timed quiz that will test your knowledge of coding, and a few Easter Eggs. You have 180 seconds to complete the quiz, and your score is determined by the number of seconds remaining at the conclusion of the quiz. Each incorrect answer will deduct 15 seconds from the time remaining. Your most recent score will be logged with your initials at the conclusion of the quiz. Go ahead and give it a try!
